An Estate Agent You Can Trust

South Coast Estates was founded in Poole in 1987 with a simple principle: to provide honest, expert property advice to the people of Dorset. Over nearly four decades, that commitment has never wavered.

As an independent agency, we are not driven by corporate targets or national quotas. Every property we take on receives the full attention of our experienced team — people who live and work in the communities they serve.

Whether you are buying your first home in Poole, selling a family house in Bournemouth, or looking to let in the Purbecks, we bring the same dedication and local knowledge that has earned us the trust of thousands of clients over the years.
